Support – 3 Pages Daily

Thank you for using 3 Pages Daily. We’re here to help!

Common Questions

  • How do I export my notes? Tap the export icon on any page to download as PDF or TXT.
  • Where is my data stored? Everything is saved locally on your device. We do not collect or upload your data.
  • Why am I limited to 3 pages per day? Free users can write 3 pages daily. You can unlock more with a Pro or Plus upgrade.
